# FreePace

## GPS Running & Route Recorder

Track your runs with precision GPS mapping, live pace and cadence data, cool-down mode, route checkpoints, and detailed split analysis — all in a clean, battery-efficient interface designed for runners.

---

## Features

### Ready When You Are

![Landing Page](freepace/landing_page.png)

Open the app and tap Start. Your current location is shown on the map with your last run stats at a glance. No sign-up, no setup — just run.

### Live Run Tracking

![Active Run](freepace/in_run_view.png)

Your route is drawn in real time on the map with live updates on distance, pace, elapsed time, and elevation gain. Pause and resume freely — FreePace keeps accurate records even through breaks.

### Cool-Down Mode

![Cool-Down Mode](freepace/cool_down_mode.png)

Tap Cool Down mid-run to separate your warm-down walk from your running stats. Toggle between Running Only or Total stats on the fly. Your run summary automatically shows both sections.

### Run Summary

![Run Summary — Route & Splits](freepace/run_summary_route.png)

Review your route on the map, elevation profile, and split-by-split breakdown after every run. Each split card shows pace, elevation change, cadence, and distance.

![Run Summary — Metrics & Weather](freepace/run_summary_metrics.png)

See your running vs. total stats side by side, along with weather conditions captured at the start of your run.

### Route Analysis & Checkpoints

![Route Analysis](freepace/route_analysis.png)

Track your performance over time on saved routes. Drop checkpoint pins and compare splits across runs with trend lines and multiple metrics — pace, duration, elevation, cadence, weather, and more.

### Run History

![Run History](freepace/run_history.png)

Browse your complete run history with distance, duration, and pace for every run. Filter and search to find any past run.

### Saved Routes

![Saved Routes](freepace/saved_routes.png)

Save frequently-run routes with custom names. See run counts, checkpoint pins, and route maps at a glance.

### Data Explorer

![Data Explorer](freepace/data_explorer.png)

Chart any metric against any other across all your runs. Filter by date, route, distance, and more to uncover trends in your training.

### Settings & Customization

![Settings — Units, Map & Audio Cues](freepace/settings_1.png)

Choose imperial or metric units, customize split distances, toggle map time markers, and configure audio cues to announce your pace at each split or on a time interval.

![Settings — Appearance, Export & Offline Maps](freepace/settings_2.png)

Switch between light, dark, or system appearance. Export all runs as GPX files. Cache map tiles for offline running.

## Why FreePace?

- **No account required** — open the app and run
- **No subscriptions** — every feature is free, forever
- **No ads** — a clean experience with zero interruptions
- **No data collection** — everything stays on your device
- **Battery efficient** — GPS is active only during runs

---

## System Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later
- Location Services enabled
- Motion & Fitness permissions for cadence tracking
