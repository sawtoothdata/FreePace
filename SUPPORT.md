# FreePace — Support

## About

FreePace is a GPS running tracker for iPhone. Track your runs with live mapping, pace splits, cadence tracking, audio cues, and offline maps — all with your data stored locally on your device.

## Contact

For support, bug reports, or feature requests, please [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/sawtoothdata/FreePace/issues).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why does FreePace need "Always" location access?
FreePace uses background location to continue tracking your run when the screen is locked or the app is in the background. If you only grant "While Using" access, tracking may pause when you lock your phone.

### Does FreePace drain my battery?
FreePace uses high-accuracy GPS during active runs, which does consume battery. The app automatically reduces location accuracy when a run is paused and stops GPS entirely when no run is active. For a typical 1-hour run, expect roughly 5-10% battery usage.

### How do I export my run data?
Open any run from your history, then tap the Export button to share it as a GPX file. You can also export all runs at once from Settings.

### Can I import runs from other apps?
Yes. Tap the Import button in the History tab to import GPX files from other running apps or devices.

### Where is my data stored?
All data is stored locally on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded to the cloud. If you delete the app, all data is removed.

### Does FreePace work without an internet connection?
Yes. Run tracking, splits, cadence, and audio cues all work offline. If you have cached map tiles for your area, maps will also display offline. Weather data requires an internet connection and will simply be unavailable if offline.

### Why is cadence not showing?
Cadence requires the device's motion sensor (pedometer). Make sure you have granted motion permissions when prompted. Cadence tracking is available on iPhones with a built-in pedometer.

### How do I change between miles and kilometers?
Go to Settings and change the Unit System between Imperial (miles) and Metric (kilometers).

## System Requirements

- iPhone running iOS 17.0 or later
- Location Services enabled
- Motion & Fitness permissions (for cadence tracking)
