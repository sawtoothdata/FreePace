# FreePace — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 10, 2026

## Overview

FreePace ("the App") is a GPS running tracker for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App collects, how it is used, and how it is stored.

## Data Collection

### Precise Location Data
The App collects precise GPS location data to track your running route, calculate distance, pace, and elevation. Location data is collected only while you are actively recording a run. Background location access is used solely to continue tracking when the screen is locked during a run.

### Motion and Fitness Data
The App accesses your device's motion sensors (pedometer) to measure step cadence during runs.

### Weather Data
The App may request current weather conditions from Apple WeatherKit at the start of a run to display alongside your run summary.

## Data Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device only.** The App does not transmit any personal data, location data, fitness data, or usage data to any external server.

- No user accounts are created or required
- No data is uploaded to the cloud
- No data is shared with third parties
- No analytics or tracking SDKs are included
- No advertising SDKs are included

## Data Export

You may export your run data as GPX files using the in-app export feature. Exported files are shared only through methods you explicitly choose (e.g., AirDrop, email, Files app).

## Data Deletion

All run data can be deleted from within the App. Deleting the App from your device removes all stored data.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple WeatherKit to fetch weather conditions. This request is made directly to Apple's servers and is subject to [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/).

No other third-party services are used.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
